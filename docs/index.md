# Welcome to TickTock

TickTock is a modern, lightweight time-tracking application built specifically for macOS.

## Why TickTock?

TickTock is designed for users who value efficiency and minimalism. It stays out of your way while providing powerful tools to track and visualize your productivity.

## Key Features

- **Global Hotkeys**: Control your tracking from any application.
- **Visual Timeline**: See your day at a glance.
- **Smart Search**: Find and start tasks instantly.
- **Local Storage**: Your data never leaves your computer.
